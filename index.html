Reibus

Not perfectly comfortable sightseeing

The number of "Muslim" tourists visiting Japan is increasing every year. However, there are not many places of worship called “mosque” in Japan that they need, so they have concerns about their religion during the time.

Mosques are not enough

In fact, although the number of mosques in Japan has been increasing year by year, there are still only about 100 mosques in Japan, which is not enough.

Combination of a mosque and a bus

To solve this problem, we have created a "Reibus", which is a combination of a mosque and a bus, and use it as a simple place of worship at events around the country.

Providing the usual quality

You can “Wudu” with a portable shower

A simple shower on the bus can be used for "wudu," purification before worship.

No worries about cleanliness

Place a worship mat on the bus reduce concerns about hygiene.

You can get the direction

There is a compass on the bus so you can check the direction of Mecca.

Hoping a comfortable future

We hope that through this "Reibus" we can not only make sightseeing more comfortable for Muslims, but also provide opportunities for Japanese people to experience multiculturalism and increase the number of visitors to the event.
